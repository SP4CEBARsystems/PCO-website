Why The ICT Field Suits Me

I like web development, this field of engineering allows products to be developed relatively quickly, it allows tools to be made and distributed cheaply to enrich the lives of everyone who owns a computer and to allow the developer(s) to make living in the process. The ideas I have for software I want to develop, and my passion for programming, that is my motivation.

I like the simplicity and reliability offered by HTML with javascript, it allows sophiscticated online tools to be made relatively easily.

However I started again half a year ago, this time I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Hundreds of hours I have spent on it already, and the first release is getting into view.
