FAQ

    How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    Go to https://hz.mynetpay.nl/. Log in with your school account (without @hz.nl). You can upload a file at WebPrint or install the printer at MobilityPrint. Please note! You need print credit to print. For more information, go to the self-service portal.

    How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    Go to a scanner/printer. Log in (with pass or username and password). Press Scan. If you want to scan double-sided, go to the settings and set the duplex mode to 2-sided. Put your papers where they belong and press Start. Then log out again (top right).

    How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

    Go to https://webshop.hz.nl/. Log in. Then find the product you want to buy, select the option you want (e.g. color, size, quantity) and then press Add to cart. Then go to SHOPPING CART at the top. Then press checkout. Choose your payment method. Press Next and follow the instructions on your screen.

    How can you book a project space?

    To book a project space:

    1. visit portal.hz.nl
    2. then navigate to
      1. Selfservice Portal,
      2. reservations,
      3. Book a project or meeting space,
      4. Book a space,
    3. Then find a room with a timeslot that suits your needs. Select the timeslot.
    4. Fill in your group name, number of people, and perhaps some optional extra details.
    5. Press next.
    6. Press confirm.
    What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    It is not possible to park directly at the HZ at Het Groene Woud. There is a special parking space available on the premises for people with disabilities and/or chronic conditions. In the parking garage at the Kousteensedijk there are a number of spaces for visitors, employees and students of Het Groene Woud. This parking garage is an 8-minute walk from the HZ. Upon presentation of your entry ticket, you can obtain an exit ticket at the JRCZ service desk.
