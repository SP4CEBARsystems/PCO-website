Who I am

my BASIC programs (on LowresNX) my GitHub profile

I am Boaz Crezee, a 21 years old amateur programmer, runner, and christian from Vlissingen. As far as I can remember I have been interested by, thinking about, developing, drawing, and or building intricate systems, machines, and devices.

Running

I like running, from around 2014 to 2023 I have been part of an Athletics club. Zeeland is the perfect place to run thanks to its flat terrain and beautiful coastline.

I've run Zeeland's coastal marathon in 2021 and in 2022, it is a 42-kilometer run from the city of Burgh-Haemstede in Schouwen-Duiveland to the village Zoutelande in Walcheren. the last 10 kilometers are tough, yet running a marathon is the experience of a lifetime.

YouTube

I have a YouTube channel where I show and explain electrical systems in 3D sandbox games like Minecraft and Survivalcraft. Survivalcraft is about as old as Minecraft, but it has more powerful electrical components. With them, I have made mechanical printers, text screens, and other smart mechanical machines.
